❓ Help & About
Using the App
👥
Your Team — Pick your team from the top dropdown before logging visits. Each team gets its own color on the map.
📍
Log Visit — Tap the big amber button after knocking on a door. Choose what happened, add a note if needed, and hit Save.
🗺️
Filter Map — Use the "Show:" dropdown to view just one team's visits or all teams at once.
☀️
Light / Dark — Tap the sun/moon button in the top bar to switch between light mode (daytime) and dark mode (nighttime).
🌡️
Heatmap — Tap the thermometer button to see a color overlay showing which streets have the most coverage.
⊕
Re-center — Tap the crosshair button to snap the map back to your current location.
🟢
Sync status — Bottom-left shows "Synced" (connected) or "Offline" (no internet — visits still save locally and sync when you reconnect).
